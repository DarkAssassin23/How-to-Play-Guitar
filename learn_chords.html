Learning Chords

Now that the guitar is in tune, it's time to start learning. With out getting into boring theory, lets first start off by learning some basic chords in order to play a song. Some of the most popular chords to play in music are G, C, and D. You can learn how to play those chords in the video below

Now that you know a few chords, keep practicing them over and over and work on transitioning between each. The smoother the transition you can make the sooner you'll be able to put everything together and play some real songs.
